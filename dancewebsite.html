LINGDANG’S DANCE VIDEO

DANCE ASSIGNMENTS

  • Video 1 – Childhood Memory

    Set Material

    In this piece, dance and choreography have linked to my childhood memory, and memory to me, is the perception of the passing of time. When talking about time, the first thing that came into my mind was the unstoppable flowing water. Thus, to explore my childhood memory, I dive into the river of memory and float with the water flow. All the movements in this piece are trying to express the feelings of floating with a river and trying to hold onto memories. The clockwise spin in the end represents the irreversibility of time and no matter how hard I tried to hold onto the past, life still goes on. And as the jump in the end indicates, in the process of growing up, I learned to let it go. This piece is composed of set materials

    Video 2 – Neuroplasticity

    Improvisation

    In this dance, I began by mimicking how signals travel through the nervous cells via nodes of Ranvier when the myelin sheath wraps around the axon. I then tried to mimic when the electrical signals reach the axon terminal, it is converted into chemical signals via the release of transmitters, where the synaptic vesicle proteins will be “activated” by the calcium ions. I then tried to mimic the axon-specific recycling of neurotransmitters and also how substances get into the channels when the ions channels are selectively permeable to certain ions and when the protein channels are opened when a specific ligand binds to them
